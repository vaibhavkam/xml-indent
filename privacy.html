Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy outlines how xml-indent.com collects and uses information from users of our free online XML tools (Formatter, Validator, Minifier, XPath Tester).

Data processing

All XML processing — formatting, validating, minifying, and XPath evaluation — happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript and the browser's built-in DOM parser. Your XML data is never transmitted to our servers. We do not store, log, or have access to any XML you paste into our tools.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data such as page views, country of origin, and device type. This data helps us understand how the site is used and improve it. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out.

Advertising

This site may display advertisements served by third-party networks. These networks may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.

Cookies

We do not set cookies ourselves beyond a theme preference stored in localStorage (not a cookie). Third-party services we use may set cookies on your device.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page.
